// How It Works

Three steps. Then you walk away.

01
The strategy fires

A graded breakout triggers at the close of the bar — exactly as Knightfall does, with the same A+ through C scoring and grade-based contract sizing.

02
Your service receives it

It sends the order — direction, contracts, entry, stop, and targets — to the automation service you connect (PickMyTrade, TradersPost, and others).

03
Your broker executes

The bracket lives at your broker and manages the trade. At session end it flattens automatically — and it skips holidays and half-days on its own.

The strategy emits alerts only — it does not place orders itself. Execution happens through a third-party service and broker that you configure and control. Full setup guide included on access.
