Getting Started

Just subscribed? Follow these steps in order to get full access to the FSD-X suite. The whole process takes under 10 minutes.

Step 1 — Discord Step 2 — TradingView Indicators Step 3 — Nexus Key Step 4 — You're Live
1

Claim Your Discord Access

Connect your Whop subscription to the FSD-X Discord server

After subscribing on Whop, you need to claim your Discord access so you can join the private community and receive SCOUT alerts.

  1. 01.Log into your Whop dashboard
  2. 02.Find your FSD-X membership and click Claim next to the Discord option
  3. 03.Authorize the connection with your Discord account when prompted
  4. 04.You'll automatically be added to the VIP channels in the server
Already in Discord? Make sure you're using the same Discord account you want linked. If you need help, open a ticket in the server.
Whop dashboard → Claim Discord access
2

Claim the TradingView Indicators

Add FSD-X ORB PRO and companion indicators to your TradingView account

The TradingView indicators are invite-only and need to be claimed through Whop before they appear in your TradingView scripts library.

  1. 01.Go back to your Whop dashboard and find the FSD-X membership
  2. 02.Click Claim next to the TradingView indicator option
  3. 03.Enter your TradingView username (not email — the username shown on your profile)
  4. 04.Within a few minutes the indicators will appear in your TradingView Invite-Only Scripts section
  5. 05.Search "FSD-X" in the indicator search bar to add them to your chart
Don't see them? It can take up to 5 minutes to appear. Make sure your TradingView username is entered correctly — it's case sensitive.
Whop dashboard → Claim TradingView indicators
3

Get Your Nexus & Optimizer Key

Find your license key in the Whop Software section to unlock VIP features in Nexus

FSD-X Nexus 2.0 and Strategy Optimizer Pro require a license key to unlock VIP features. Your key is found in the Software section of your Whop dashboard.

  1. 01.In your Whop dashboard, navigate to the Software tab of your FSD-X membership
  2. 02.Copy your license key
  3. 03.Open Nexus in Chrome and click the KEY button in the top right of the extension
  4. 04.Paste your key and click Activate — your tier will update to VIP instantly
Key locks to your device Your key activates on the first device you use it on. If you need to transfer to a new device, contact us in Discord or at fsdxteam@gmail.com.
Don't have Nexus installed yet? Add it to Chrome here →
Whop dashboard → Software → Copy your key
