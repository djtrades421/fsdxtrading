Default Calibration Settings

Recommended baseline settings for NQ/MNQ on the 5-minute timeframe. Find these in the FSD-X ORB PRO indicator settings panel on TradingView.

Setting Recommended Value Why It Matters
ORB Start Time 8:30 AM CT Hard-anchored to Central Time in the source code. Keep this at 8:30 regardless of your local chart timezone — the indicator handles the conversion automatically.
VWAP Alignment Filter ON (Strict) Breakouts are ignored if price isn't on the correct side of VWAP. This is a core quality filter — leaving it on keeps you aligned with session flow.
Realistic Trade Tracking ON Prevents inflated win rate stats. The dashboard won't log a second trade until the first one resolves at TP or SL — keeps your data honest.
Stop Loss Range 50 Min / 150 Max pts The hybrid SL engine uses both structure and ATR to size stops. These bounds act as safety rails — stops won't go tighter than 50 pts or wider than 150 pts.
Minimum ORB Filter 45 pts Skips setups when the opening range is too tight to produce meaningful breakouts. Anything below 45 points on MNQ typically produces false signals.
Min Grade to Trade C or better The grading system scores each setup A+ through F. For best results focus on A+/A/B+ trades. C and above is the minimum threshold to consider entering.
